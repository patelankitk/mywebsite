Mortgage Extra Payment Calculator

Description of the project.

The Mortgage Extra payments calculator calculates the time and interest saved paying off a mortgage, when making regular monthly overpayments.

It’s a project on ASP.net WebForm, it calculates the interest you can save over the complete term of your mortgage by making extra monthly payment and can also calculate the how early you can pay off your mortgage. It gives complete detail figures of all the aspects of your mortgage. Additionally, it gives a graph which shows your old term(years) and new term(years) after additional payment.

Interest Rates - The calculator assumes that interest rates will remain the same.

Technologies used:

• ASP.NET - Web-forms

• C#

• Bootstrap

• HTML

• CSS

Future Scope.

• Calculating Monthly Amortization Schedule

• Adding one-off payment option.

• Adding the option of allowing user to add variable interest rate.

• Adding features like generating the PDF format of the Amortization figures / Email the Amortization figures or Print the figures.
